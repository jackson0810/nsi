Naval Systems Incorporated (NSI), a Veteran owned small business with a Manufacturing, Repair, Overhaul and Logistics (MRO&L) Division located in Jacksonville, Florida. We specialize in the remanufacturing and rework of aerospace components and can provide a complete range of supply chain management functions and services. We are a FAA certified provider of Avionics, Instruments and Test Equipment repair and overhaul. Our experiences in the role of third party integrator are reflected in current performance under several Performance Based Logistics contracts. NSI MRO&L provides the highest quality products and services at competitive prices, being registered to ISO9000:2000 and AS9100:2004 international quality standards. We are members of the National Society of Safety Professionals and the NE FL Safety Council. Our dedicated senior management team brings over 150 years of military and business operations experience to each and every task as well as an uncompromising devotion to performance excellence.


Mission: To create and deliver products and services of unmatched value that answer our clients most pressing needs!
